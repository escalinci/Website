Wedding Videography

Making a record of one of the most important days of someone's life- there can be nothing as sensitive and individual, and so it should never be assumed that any two weddings would be best filmed the same way.

With this in mind, I can offer anything from a short video that gives an impression of the day, to a longer video, using two cameras, including all speeches and readings in full, and messages for the couple from guests.

With my background in documentary work I am able to fit the environment rather than making the environment fit me. As good as you'd want your record to be, it can't overshadow the day. Anyway, the happy couple from the video above can put it better than I can!

"We really cannot express how much we love both videos - they are so fantastic and really show off the wonderful day we had! They have made us so happy and we are very very grateful."

"We are both so delighted that you have done this for us and cannot thank you enough, they are beautiful pieces of art but also mean so much more to us. We could not be everywhere at once on the day so it is positively thrilling to see the bits we missed as well as to remember the parts we enjoyed." -Dan and Elizabeth
